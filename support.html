Help & FAQ

Need assistance using the app? Start here.

  • How do I start? Open the app and select your state to begin learning about your local voting process.
  • Is this app nonpartisan? Yes. We provide neutral, fact-based guidance.
  • Can I register to vote in the app? We reference official voter registration websites, but we do not collect or transmit personal data. As such there is no way to register directly in the app
