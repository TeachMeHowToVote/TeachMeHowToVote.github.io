Help & FAQ

Have questions, or need assistance using the app? Start here.


  • How do I start? Open the app, answer the questions, and start with the guides tab.

  • Is this app nonpartisan? Yes. We provide neutral, fact-based guidance, not linked to any individual candidate or party.

  • Can I register to vote in the app? There is no way to directly register in the app. You can access your states voting website from the Local tab and register from there.
